A Traditional Italian Staple

Pasta is a widely loved food made from wheat flour and water, sometimes mixed with eggs. It originated in Italy and comes in many shapes and sizes such as Spaghetti, Penne, and Fusilli. Pasta is usually boiled and served with a variety of sauces like tomato, cream, or pesto. It is popular around the world because it is versatile, quick to prepare, and can be combined with many ingredients such as vegetables, meat, or seafood

  • Italian Origin
  • Wheat Dough
  • Many Shapes
  • Boiled Dish
  1. Boil Water – Bring salted water to a boil
  2. Cook Pasta – Add pasta and cook until tender.
  3. Prepare Sauce – Heat or cook your preferred sauce
  4. Combine Dish – Mix pasta with sauce and serve
