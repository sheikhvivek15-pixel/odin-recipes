Pizza: A Famous Italian Dish

Pizza is a popular dish made with a flat round dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various ingredients. It originated in Naples, Italy and has become one of the most loved foods worldwide. A traditional version is Pizza Margherita, which includes tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil. Pizza is baked in a hot oven and can be customized with many toppings such as vegetables, meat, or seafood.

  • Italian Origin
  • Dough Base
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Baked Dish
  1. Prepare Dough – Roll out the pizza dough into a round shape
  2. Add Toppings – Spread tomato sauce, cheese, and toppings
  3. Bake Pizza – Place in a hot oven until crust is golden
  4. Serve Hot – Slice and enjoy the pizza
